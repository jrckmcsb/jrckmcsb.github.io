Account Takeover on Edmodo via exposed password reset token

Edmodo is a EdTech websites that allows the user to communicate with the teachers,parents and students. On my bug bounty journey, I still remember the first time that I managed to recieve the swag from Edmodo after finding my first bug on the website, the bug allows me to download any .docx file from the chatbox of other users, after some months of doing some security testing on Edmodo, they changed their rules and starts to give monetary compensation/bounty instead of swags.

Improving my status

After Edmodo changed their rules, I decided to invest my time on learning some new bugs,techniques and other stuffs, after a short period of time I learned other stuffs from recon phase, different tools and other bugs. After the bugs are get confirmed by Edmodo Security Team, they send some bounty on me and it helps me to invest on my equipment from laptop and owning a high specs computer, the bounty also helps me to provide some basic needs for my parents and siblings, a lot of time pass but still I didn't know the forgot-password bug

Account Takeover

I remember that night, I am watching a talk from Youtuber called nahamsec, he's interviewing ngalonc on that talk and they talk about the leakage of password reset token, after watching it I decide to try the new technique that I learned, after testing it I learned that all this time I am doing security testing with an authenticated users but not with unauthenticated users and the bug can really used to breach the company. I analyzed the request and response on Burpsuite and I confirm that the password reset token is exposed. I immediately create a report and a proof of concept for the exploit of that vulnerability, after some time Edmodo fixed the vulnerability

After they confirm it they sent to me a 500$ bounty

On that day I learned the importance of checking the request and the response
